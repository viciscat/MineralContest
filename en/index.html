Mineral Contest

The best team-based multiplayer Minecraft game mode, and we're totally NOT biased!

What is the Mineral Contest?

The teams

The Mineral Contest is played with 4 teams. To maximise the amount of fun, we advise you to play with 4 players in each team. However, there is no minimum number of players required, so you can also play 1 vs 1, but in that case, the fun factor will be very low. To start the game, right-click on the compass and select the colour of your choice.

The classes

When the game starts, you have 45sec to choose a class from among :

  • Agile (feather): You move 20% faster and you don't take any fall damage.
  • Worker (gold): You increase the points your team win by 25% but you only have 5 hearts.
  • Robust (armor): You have 12 hearts and the damage you take is reduced by 12%
  • Warrior (sword): You deal 25% more damage but you only have 8 hearts.
  • Miner (pickaxe): The ores you mine auto-smelt! But you only have 2 rows in your inventory.

If you don't choose one time, RNGesus shall choose for you

Start of the game

When the game starts, you will have an iron armor, a stone sword, a bow and 32 arrows and 16 steaks! You will get those items back when you respawn, but they don't drop when you die!

... What now?

Take a your steak, and beat up mother nature! Craft a crafting table, make a pickaxe and go mining some ore to gain some points!

How do I win?

To win the game, mine ores, smelt them and take them to the Ender Chest in you castle! The shinier it is, the more points you earn. Note that redstone removes 1 point from all the other teams!
